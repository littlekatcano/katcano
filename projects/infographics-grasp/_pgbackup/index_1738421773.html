Objective: Design a series of motivational and business-related ads for the book "Million Dollar Shot" by Dale Mochan.

Process & Tactics:

  • Understanding the Book: Gained insights into the key themes and messages of "Million Dollar Shot".
  • Conceptualization: Brainstormed and sketched initial design concepts to reflect the book's themes of risk management, visionary mindset, leadership, and innovation.
  • Design Execution: Created a cohesive set of 15 ads with a consistent color scheme of red, black, and white. The colors and overall design were set following the parameters of the brand guidelines. Each ad features bold text and images of the book, along with motivational messages.
  • Feedback & Refinement: Refined the ads to ensure they effectively communicated the intended messages and were visually engaging.
  • Final Delivery: Ensured all ads were of high quality and ready for both digital and print distribution.

Outcome: Effective Promotion: The ads successfully attracted attention and conveyed the key messages of "Million Dollar Shot", contributing to increased interest and engagement with the book.
