    Senior Graphic Designer

Objective

To create a visually engaging and informative video for the WHOOP App, emphasizing its capacity measurement features and encouraging users to track their fitness and health.

Color Scheme

  • Primary Colors: Black and white. The black color conveys a sense of sophistication and strength, while the white background ensures a clean and crisp look.
  • Accent Colors: Elite gold accents to add a touch of luxury and excellence, complementing the primary color scheme.

Typography

  • Headings: Bold and large font size in black to make them stand out and grab attention. A sporty and sharp sans-serif font to ensure a modern and dynamic appearance.
  • Body Text: Smaller font size in grey for readability and to maintain a professional look. Consistent use of sporty and sharp sans-serif fonts ensures uniformity and clarity.

Layout

  • Consistent Structure: The video follows a consistent structure to maintain brand cohesion. Clear sections with headings, body text, and visuals ensure easy navigation.
  • White Space: Ample use of white space to prevent clutter and enhance readability. This also helps in creating a clean and professional look.
  • Alignment: Center alignment for titles and key visuals to maintain balance and harmony. Left alignment for body text to ensure readability.

Imagery

  • High-Quality Visuals: Use of high-quality visuals of the app interface, athletes, and related icons to visually represent the app's features and benefits. Visuals are carefully selected to convey the reliability and professionalism of WHOOP.
  • Consistent Style: Visuals follow a consistent style and color tone to maintain uniformity across the video content.

Visual Hierarchy

  • Font Sizes and Colors: Use of different font sizes and colors to establish a clear visual hierarchy. Important information like headings and key points are highlighted using larger font sizes and bold colors.
  • Bullet Points: Use of bullet points to list key features and benefits for easy readability and quick reference.

Branding

  • Logo Placement: Prominent placement of the WHOOP logo to reinforce brand identity and recognition.
  • Taglines and Slogans: Inclusion of taglines and slogans to communicate the brand message effectively and engage the audience.

Outcome

The art direction successfully creates a visually appealing and informative video that reflects the reliability and professionalism of the WHOOP App. The clean and modern design, combined with carefully selected typography, colors, and visuals, ensures that the video is not only informative but also visually engaging. This encourages potential users to engage with the content and consider using the WHOOP App to measure their capacity and track their fitness and health.
