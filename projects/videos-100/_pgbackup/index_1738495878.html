  • Role
    Senior Graphic Designer

Objective

To create an engaging and informative promotional flyer for the "Sensational Voyage" by Resorts World Cruises, highlighting various wellness activities and events available on the cruise from 2 to 16 November 2022.

Color Scheme

  • Primary Colors: Light blue and white. The light blue color evokes a sense of tranquility and relaxation, aligning with the wellness theme, while the white background ensures a clean and refreshing look.
  • Accent Colors: Subtle use of green and other complementary colors to add visual interest without overpowering the primary color scheme.

Typography

  • Headings: Bold and large font size in light blue to make them stand out and grab attention. A sans-serif font like 'Helvetica Neue' ensures a modern and clean appearance.
  • Body Text: Smaller font size in grey for readability and to maintain a professional look. Consistent use of sans-serif fonts ensures uniformity and clarity.

Layout

  • Consistent Structure: The flyer follows a consistent structure to maintain brand cohesion. Clear sections with headings, body text, and images ensure easy navigation.
  • White Space: Ample use of white space to prevent clutter and enhance readability. This also helps in creating a clean and refreshing look.
  • Alignment: Center alignment for titles and key images to maintain balance and harmony. Left alignment for body text to ensure readability.

Imagery

  • High-Quality Images: Use of high-quality images of wellness activities, such as yoga, sound healing, and fitness classes, to visually represent the theme of the cruise. Images are carefully selected to convey relaxation, health, and rejuvenation.
  • Consistent Style: Images follow a consistent style and color tone to maintain uniformity across the promotional materials.

Visual Hierarchy

  • Font Sizes and Colors: Use of different font sizes and colors to establish a clear visual hierarchy. Important information like headings and key points are highlighted using larger font sizes and bold colors.
  • Bullet Points: Use of bullet points to list activities and details for easy readability and quick reference.

Branding

  • Logo Placement: Prominent placement of the Resorts World Cruises logo to reinforce brand identity and recognition.
  • Taglines and Slogans: Inclusion of taglines and slogans to communicate the brand message effectively and engage the audience.

Outcome

The art direction successfully creates an engaging and informative promotional flyer that reflects the wellness and relaxation theme of the "Sensational Voyage" by Resorts World Cruises. The clean and modern design, combined with carefully selected typography, colors, and imagery, ensures that the flyer is not only informative but also visually appealing. This encourages potential customers to engage with the content and consider participating in the wellness activities during their cruise.
