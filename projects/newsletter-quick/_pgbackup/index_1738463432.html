  • Role
    Multimedia Graphic Designer

Objective: Design an engaging promotional graphic to highlight the activities attendees can look forward to at an event.

Process & Tactics:

  • Understanding the Event: Gained insights into the key elements and themes of the event to incorporate them into the graphic design.
  • Conceptualization: Brainstormed and sketched initial design concepts featuring festive elements and bright colors to attract attention.
  • Design Execution: Created a vibrant promotional graphic with a pink background and colorful abstract shapes. Listed three activities that attendees can look forward to:
    1. Panel Discussion: "Join 4 amazing influencers for an hour-long panel with extended Q&A time!"
    2. Complimentary Drink Ticket: "Enjoy a delicious frosé on us" with a cocktail emoji.
    3. Goody Bags: "Filled with some of our ✨favorite✨ brands" with sparkle emojis.
    The colors and overall design were set following the parameters of the brand guidelines.
  • Feedback & Refinement: Refined the graphic to ensure it met the promotional goals and brand guidelines.
  • Final Delivery: Ensured the promotional graphic was of high quality and ready for distribution.

UI/UX Principles:

  • Consistency: Ensured visual and functional consistency throughout the design, using the brand's colors, fonts, and styling.
  • Hierarchy: Emphasized important elements such as activity titles using size, color, and placement.
  • Readability: Chose legible fonts and appropriate font sizes to ensure the text is easy to read.
  • Accessibility: Ensured color contrast and clickable elements are accessible to all users.

Art Direction:

  • Visual Appeal: Used a clean and modern design to create a professional and engaging look.
  • Color Scheme: Incorporated the brand's colors, primarily pink and white, to maintain brand consistency.
  • Imagery: Selected high-quality abstract shapes and icons to visually represent the event activities.
  • Layout: Designed a clear and structured layout to guide the user's attention and enhance readability.

Outcome: Engaging Promotion: The promotional graphic successfully highlighted the event activities, attracting attention and generating interest among attendees.
