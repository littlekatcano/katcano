  • Aegis Live Concert in NYC

    Print

  • UI/UX

    Print

  • Role
    Multimedia Graphic Designer

Objective: Design an eye-catching promotional poster and print assets for the Aegis live concert in New York City, emphasizing event details, ticket information, and available packages.

Process & Tactics:

  • Understanding the Event: Gained insights into the concert details, including date, time, venue, ticket prices, and available packages to incorporate them into the design.
  • Conceptualization: Brainstormed and sketched initial design concepts featuring the band's presence and the New York City skyline to highlight the concert's location.
  • Design Execution: Created a visually appealing poster with the band members in the background and the New York City skyline. The text includes event details such as the date (October 9, 2015, 7:30 PM), venue (BMCC Tribeca Performing Arts Center), and ticket prices ($120, $100, $80, and $30 for a meet and greet pass). Provided information on where to buy tickets in person and online, along with contact information for inquiries. Highlighted different packages available for concert attendees, such as VIP access with backstage passes. The colors and overall design were set following the parameters of the brand guidelines.
  • Feedback & Refinement: Refined the poster and print assets to ensure they met the promotional goals and brand guidelines.
  • Final Delivery: Ensured the poster and print assets were of high quality and ready for distribution. Created additional print materials such as flyers, t-shirts, tarpaulins, pull-up banners, ballpens, and posters to enhance the concert promotion.
  • Booking Website: Developed a booking website where attendees can purchase tickets and choose from different packages, including VIP packages with backstage access. Ensured the website was user-friendly and provided a seamless booking experience.

UI/UX Principles:

  • Consistency: Ensured visual and functional consistency throughout the design, using the brand's colors, fonts, and styling.
  • Hierarchy: Emphasized important elements such as the event details, ticket prices, and package information using size, color, and placement.
  • Readability: Chose legible fonts and appropriate font sizes to ensure the text is easy to read.
  • User-Friendly Booking: Developed a booking website that provided a seamless and intuitive user experience, allowing attendees to easily purchase tickets and select packages.

Art Direction:

  • Visual Appeal: Used a clean and modern design to create a professional and engaging look.
  • Color Scheme: Incorporated the brand's colors, primarily black, white, and red, to maintain brand consistency.
  • Imagery: Selected high-quality images of the band members and the New York City skyline to visually represent the concert's location and the band's presence.
  • Layout: Designed a clear and structured layout to guide the viewer's attention and enhance readability.

Outcome: Effective Promotion: The poster and print assets successfully highlighted the event details, ticket information, and available packages, attracting attention and generating interest in the Aegis live concert in New York City.
