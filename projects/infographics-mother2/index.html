  • Mother's Day at Takashimaya Giveaway

    Infographics

  • Key Visual

  • Role
    Senior Graphic Designer

Objective: Design a festive and informative advertisement for Mother's Day giveaway event at Takashimaya.

Process & Tactics:

  • Understanding the Event: Gained insights into the key elements and themes of Mother's Day to incorporate them into the ad design.
  • Conceptualization: Brainstormed and sketched initial design concepts featuring floral elements and symbolic meanings of flowers.
  • Design Execution: Created a vibrant advertisement with flowers and their symbolic meanings, such as tulips, orchids, roses, hydrangea, lilies, and carnations. The central part of the ad features the text "Celebrate Mother's Day at Takashimaya" in a decorative font, with a banner reading "Giveaway." The colors and overall design were set following the parameters of the brand guidelines.
  • Feedback & Refinement: Refined the ad to ensure it met the promotional goals and brand guidelines.
  • Final Delivery: Ensured the advertisement was of high quality and ready for both digital and print distribution.

Outcome: Festive Engagement: The advertisement successfully captured the festive spirit of Mother's Day and attracted attention, contributing to increased participation in the giveaway event.
