Objective

To create a visually appealing and festive greeting card for Easter, emphasizing the joy and warmth of the holiday, and conveying heartfelt wishes.

Color Scheme

  • Primary Colors: Pink and white. The pink background conveys a sense of warmth and celebration, while the white accents ensure a clean and elegant look.
  • Accent Colors: Pastel colors for the Easter eggs to add vibrancy and highlight key elements.

Typography

  • Font Style: Cursive and decorative fonts for a whimsical and festive appearance.
  • Headings: Large cursive font in white to make them stand out and grab attention.
  • Body Text: Smaller cursive font in white for readability and to maintain a warm and friendly look. Consistent use of decorative fonts ensures uniformity and charm.

Layout

  • Consistent Structure: The greeting card follows a consistent structure to maintain brand cohesion. Clear sections with headings, body text, and images ensure easy navigation.
  • White Space: Ample use of white space to prevent clutter and enhance readability. This also helps in creating a clean and elegant look.
  • Alignment: Center alignment for titles and key images to maintain balance and harmony. Left alignment for body text to ensure readability.

Imagery

  • High-Quality Images: Use of high-quality images of bunnies, floral patterns, and Easter eggs to visually represent the holiday's theme. Images are carefully selected to convey the joy and celebration of Easter.
  • Consistent Style: Images follow a consistent style and color tone to maintain uniformity across the greeting card.

Visual Hierarchy

  • Font Sizes and Colors: Use of different font sizes and colors to establish a clear visual hierarchy. Important information like headings and key points are highlighted using larger font sizes and bold colors.
  • Bullet Points: Use of bullet points to list key messages and wishes for easy readability and quick reference.

Branding

  • Logo Placement: Prominent placement of the sender's name or logo to reinforce identity and recognition.
  • Taglines and Slogans: Inclusion of taglines and slogans to communicate the holiday message effectively and engage the audience.

Outcome

The art direction successfully creates a visually appealing and festive greeting card that reflects the joy and warmth of Easter. The whimsical and decorative design, combined with carefully selected typography, colors, and imagery, ensures that the greeting card is not only informative but also visually engaging. This encourages recipients to feel the holiday spirit and appreciate the heartfelt wishes.
