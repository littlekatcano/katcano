  • Resorts World Cruises Promotional Flyer

    Print

  • Flyer

  • Role
    Senior Graphic Designer

Objective: Design an eye-catching promotional flyer for Resorts World Cruises, highlighting various cruise packages and offers.

Process & Tactics:

  • Understanding the Promotion: Gained insights into the cruise packages, sailing periods, blackout dates, and pricing to incorporate them into the flyer design.
  • Conceptualization: Brainstormed and sketched initial design concepts featuring the cruise ship and promotional details to attract attention.
  • Design Execution: Created a visually appealing flyer with the following details:
    • Itinerary:
    • 2N (Wed) | 3N (Sun)
    • 2 Oct to 16 Nov 2022
    • 2N (Wed) | 2N (Fri) | 3N (Sun)
    • Jan to Apr 2023
    • 5N (Sun)
    • 12 Mar 2023
    • Sailing Period: 2 Oct to 16 Nov 2022 (Extended to 5-Night & Jan to Apr 2023)
    • Black Out Dates: 23 Oct, 24 Oct, 13 Nov, 14 Nov, 20 Nov & 18 Dec 2022, 20 - 23 Jan, 5 & 6 Feb, 3 Mar, 7, 8, 19 - 24 Apr 2023
    • Discounts: 3rd / 4th Pax at 50% of below fare; Infant fare at 25% of below fare
    • Pricing:
    • 2N (Wed/Sun)
    • Cabin Category | Per Cabin Fare for 2 Pax
    • Palace Deluxe Premium | Wed $1,699 | Sun $2,419
    • Palace Deluxe Suite | Wed $1,599 | Sun $2,309
    • Palace Suite | Wed $1,499 | Sun $2,199
    • 5N (Sun)
    • Cabin Category | Per Cabin Fare for 2 Pax
    • Palace Deluxe Premium | $3,499
    • Palace Deluxe Suite | $3,199
    • Palace Suite | $2,749
  • The colors and overall design were set following the parameters of the brand guidelines.
  • Feedback & Refinement: Refined the flyer to ensure it met the promotional goals and brand guidelines.
  • Final Delivery: Ensured the flyer was of high quality and ready for print distribution.

Outcome: Effective Promotion: The flyer successfully highlighted the cruise packages and promotional offers, attracting attention and generating interest in booking a cruise holiday with Resorts World Cruises.
