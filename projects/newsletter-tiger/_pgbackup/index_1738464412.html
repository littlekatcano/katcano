  • Role
    Multimedia Graphic Designer

Objective: Design an engaging promotional advertisement for skincare gift guides, highlighting suitable products for various skin types.

Process & Tactics:

  • Understanding the Products: Gained insights into the skincare products suitable for various skin types to incorporate them into the ad design.
  • Conceptualization: Brainstormed and sketched initial design concepts to effectively communicate the benefits of the skincare products.
  • Design Execution: Created a visually appealing advertisement with the headline "Skincare Gift Guides." The main text reads: "The skin is an organ, so it's important to take care of it. These products are suitable for virtually all skin types, so no matter which friend you call you'll find something to love." Included a call-to-action message: "Check Out our 2018 Gift Guide to Learn More." The colors and overall design were set following the parameters of the brand guidelines.
  • Feedback & Refinement: Refined the advertisement to ensure it met the promotional goals and brand guidelines.
  • Final Delivery: Ensured the advertisement was of high quality and ready for both digital and print distribution.

UI/UX Principles:

  • Consistency: Ensured visual and functional consistency throughout the design, using the brand's colors, fonts, and styling.
  • Hierarchy: Emphasized important elements such as the headline and call-to-action using size, color, and placement.
  • Readability: Chose legible fonts and appropriate font sizes to ensure the text is easy to read.
  • Accessibility: Ensured color contrast and clickable elements are accessible to all users.

Art Direction:

  • Visual Appeal: Used a clean and modern design to create a professional and engaging look.
  • Color Scheme: Incorporated the brand's colors, primarily pink and white, to maintain brand consistency.
  • Imagery: Selected high-quality images to visually represent the skincare products and their benefits.
  • Layout: Designed a clear and structured layout to guide the user's attention and enhance readability.

Outcome: Effective Promotion: The advertisement successfully highlighted the benefits of the skincare gift guides, attracting attention and generating interest in the recommended products.
