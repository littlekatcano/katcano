    Senior Graphic Designer

Objective: Design engaging ads for a giveaway event at the Baby Fair to attract parents and expecting families.

Process & Tactics:

  • Understanding the Event: Worked closely with the team to capture the essence of the giveaway event and understand the target audience.
  • Conceptualization: Generated creative concepts featuring the giveaway prizes, including a Bugaboo Butterfly Complete stroller and a Cler Liingo Infant Car Seat.
  • Design Execution: Designed an eye-catching banner and promotional materials that clearly showcased the prizes and event dates.
  • Feedback & Refinement: Incorporated feedback from stakeholders to refine the design and ensure alignment with brand guidelines and event goals.
  • Final Delivery: Ensured all deliverables were of high quality and launched the ads across various media channels.

Outcome: Successful Campaign: The giveaway event generated significant interest and participation, contributing to the overall success of the Baby Fair.
