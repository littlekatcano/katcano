  • Key Visual

  • Role
    Senior Graphic Designer

Objective: Design an informative and visually appealing advertisement highlighting the capabilities of RZ/FIVE MPUs for IoT and industrial gateways.

Process & Tactics:

  • Understanding the Product: Gained insights into the key features and applications of the RZ/FIVE MPUs to incorporate them into the ad design.
  • Conceptualization: Brainstormed and sketched initial design concepts showcasing the technological advancements and industrial applications of the MPUs.
  • Design Execution: Created a visually engaging advertisement with a stylized Wi-Fi symbol composed of various technological and industrial scenes, including a robotic arm, a smart home interface, a circuit board, an electric vehicle, and solar panels. The background features a cityscape with digital elements and clouds. The text on the ad reads: "OUR NEW RZ/FIVE MPUs ARE A POWERHOUSE FOR IOT AND INDUSTRIAL GATEWAYS." The colors and overall design were set following the parameters of the brand guidelines.
  • Feedback & Refinement: Refined the ad to ensure it met the promotional goals and brand guidelines.
  • Final Delivery: Ensured the advertisement was of high quality and ready for both digital and print distribution.

Outcome: Effective Promotion: The advertisement successfully highlighted the capabilities of RZ/FIVE MPUs, attracting attention and generating interest in their applications for IoT and industrial gateways.
