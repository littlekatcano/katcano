    Senior Graphic Designer

Objective: Design an eye-catching advertisement for Resorts World Cruises, promoting the "Genting Dream" cruise ship and its December 2023 sailings to and from Kuala Lumpur with a special offer for Lebaran 2023.

Process & Tactics:

  • Understanding the Campaign: Gained insights into the key selling points and target audience for the December 2023 sailings.
  • Conceptualization: Brainstormed and sketched initial design concepts featuring the cruise ship and attractions of Kuala Lumpur and Penang.
  • Design Execution: Designed a visually appealing advertisement with a large cruise ship and several smaller circular images showcasing various attractions and amenities. The colors and overall design were set following the parameters of the brand guidelines.
  • Feedback & Refinement: Refined the ad to ensure it met the campaign goals and brand guidelines.
  • Final Delivery: Ensured the advertisement was of high quality and ready for both digital and print distribution.

Outcome: Effective Promotion: The advertisement successfully attracted attention and generated interest in the December 2023 sailings, contributing to increased bookings.
