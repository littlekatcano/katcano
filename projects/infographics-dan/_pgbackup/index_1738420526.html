  • Resorts World Cruises Advertisement

    Infographics

  • Key Visual

  • Role
    Senior Graphic Designer

Objective: Design an eye-catching advertisement for Resorts World Cruises, promoting the "Genting Dream" cruise ship and its December 2022 sailings to and from Kuala Lumpur with prices starting from $109 per person.

Process & Tactics:

  • Understanding the Campaign: Collaborated with the team to understand the key selling points and target audience for the December 2022 sailings.
  • Conceptualization: Brainstormed and sketched initial design concepts featuring the cruise ship and iconic landmarks of Kuala Lumpur, such as the Petronas Twin Towers.
  • Design Execution: Designed a visually appealing advertisement with a clear message, highlighting the promotional price and including the URL for more information.
  • Feedback & Refinement: Sent initial designs to stakeholders and incorporated feedback to ensure the ad met brand guidelines and campaign goals.
  • Final Delivery: Ensured the advertisement was of high quality and ready for both digital and print distribution.

Outcome: Effective Promotion: The advertisement successfully attracted attention and generated interest in the December 2022 sailings, contributing to increased bookings.
